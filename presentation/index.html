What is the Scrum?
The Scrum project management system is based on five values:
  • Openness
  • Courage
  • Respect
  • Focus
  • Commitment

Who is on the Scrum Team?

Product owner

a person who uderstands what clients need, controls, to overall vision of the project and it's goals

Scrum master

one of the team members who is responsible for promoting Scrum and ensuring Scrum is being implemented correctly of the project

Development team

the development team works on creating the product

Scrum Events

Preliminary planning

This stage: settings goals, defining the production vision

Planning

During this stage, team members work together to plan the sprint and select features ro include in the backlog

Sprint

a sprint is a time-box of one mounth or less, during which the team developers a "done" product increment

Retrospective

analysis of the sprint results together with the Scrum team, during which errors are analyzed and suggestions for improving the work are put forward

In summary

The Scrum framework helps teams of developers, marketers, engineers, and others work quickly and efficiently on a large number of projects. Constantly improving your product helps you easily adapt to any changes in the market or your customer base.
